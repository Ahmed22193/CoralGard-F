Protecting Our Oceans, One Reef at a Time

CoralGuard is an AI-powered platform dedicated to coral reef conservation, providing tools and resources for researchers and the public to monitor, protect, and restore these vital ecosystems.

Our Mission

To empower researchers with advanced AI tools for reef monitoring and analysis, while engaging the public through educational resources and community initiatives. Together, we can make a significant impact on coral reef conservation.

Our Vision

We envision a future where coral reefs thrive, supported by a global community committed to their protection. CoralGuard aims to be the leading platform for coral reef conservation, fostering collaboration and driving positive change for our oceans.

About Us

CoralGuard was founded with a passion for ocean conservation. Our team combines expertise in marine biology, artificial intelligence, and community engagement to create innovative solutions for coral reef protection. We believe that through collaboration, technology, and education, we can safeguard these vital ecosystems for future generations.

Research

Supporting scientists with cutting-edge tools for reef monitoring and analysis.

Awareness

Educating the public and raising awareness about coral reef conservation.

Community

Building a global network of individuals dedicated to saving our oceans.

Privacy Policy
